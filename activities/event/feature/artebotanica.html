Arte Botanica “Piging” is a group installation spearheaded by artist June Poticar-Dalisay to pay homage to Nature and Mother Earth, for her countless gifts to humankind. The Erehwon Art Collective together with the artists from an all-women group, KASIBULAN, came together to feature artworks made of organic materials and discards of nature. The exhibit is also a protest against the destruction of the environment. The exhibition is on going and can be viewed at the Ground Floor Lobby of Erehwon Center for the Arts Building.
